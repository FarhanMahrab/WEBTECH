November 12, 2025

OpenAI is the lead investor in a $15 million seed round in Red Queen Bio, which is trying to make sure the AI industry's defenses are growing at least as rapidly as those who seek to exploit models to harm humans, the startup's co-founder Hannu Rajaniemi said.
