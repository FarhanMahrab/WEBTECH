About the area

Located in Dubai Marina, a neighborhood in Dubai, Ciel Dubai Marina, Vignette Collection by IHG is in a shopping district and near a metro station. Mall of the Emirates and The Walk are worth checking out if shopping is on the agenda, while those wishing to experience the area's popular attractions can visit Wild Wadi Water Park and Aquaventure. Traveling with kids? Don't miss Global Village and Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo. Boat tours and swimming offer great chances to get out on the surrounding water, or you can seek out an adventure with skydiving nearby.
